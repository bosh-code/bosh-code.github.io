Identity & Values

Me & my core personal values

 I believe that I am an honest and loyal person. To give an example, a friend an I were working on a uni project together, we differed on how to approach the problem and could not come to an agreement. I honestly thought my solution was better, and he his, so we could not come to an agreement. I expressed why I felt my solution was better, however he was adamant on his approach. We went with his approach, and even though I did not agree it was the best way to tackle the problem, I give it my best and worked through the solution the same as any other.

 I could have taken charge, or been dismissive, however I felt the ethical thing to do was complete the solution his way. He cared more than I did about solving the problem his way than I did about mine.

My strengths & limitations

 Limitations: I can be very easily distracted and often focus too much time and energy on simple or more rewarding tasks, rather than priorities.
